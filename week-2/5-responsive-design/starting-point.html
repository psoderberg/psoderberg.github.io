This is the site that Kevin and I teamed up on to make a little responsive. We were both learning as we went. There were a few stumbling blocks, and a few times we should have taken a step back to think about how we should make a change rather than continue with guess-and-check, but overall it was a positive experience. It's a little tricky to figure out what someone else's stylesheet is doing. Maybe I'm too naive to notice a trick, but there was a "side-b" placeholder that didn't seem to do anything. We found it was important to actually do the math when it comes to converting pixels to percentages. Ballparking just didn't cut it.
